# RC Ready

**A free, guided companion for transferring car ownership in Maharashtra — the right way, without the runaround.**

Buying or selling a used car in India should be exciting, not stressful. But the RC (Registration Certificate) transfer process is buried under decades of rules, paperwork, and RTO jargon. Miss one document, get one form wrong, and you're stuck — or worse, legally liable for a car that's no longer yours.

RC Ready is built to fix that.

---

## What It Does

RC Ready walks you through the entire Maharashtra RC transfer process, step by step, in plain language. Whether you're the buyer or the seller, it tells you exactly what to do, what to bring, what to sign, and what to watch out for.

You answer a few questions about your situation. RC Ready does the rest — checks your readiness, scans your documents, fills your forms, and hands you a clear action plan.

No legal background needed. No middlemen. No surprises at the RTO counter.

---

## Who It's For

- **First-time buyers** of a used car who don't know where to start.
- **Sellers** who want to transfer the car out of their name properly — so toll fines, accidents, or unpaid challans never come back to haunt them.
- **Anyone** moving a car between states (interstate transfer with NOC).
- **Buyers with a loan** who need to handle hypothecation correctly.
- **Honest dealers and consultants** who want to give clients a clear checklist instead of vague reassurance.

If you've ever been told "don't worry, the agent will handle it" and felt your stomach drop — this is for you.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Most buyers and sellers walk into a used-car deal blind:

- They don't know which forms apply to their situation (it depends on whether the car has a loan, whether it's moving between states, whether the seller is a dealer, and more).
- They trust agents who skip steps to save time.
- They sign forms without checking if the engine number, chassis number, or insurance dates match.
- They walk away thinking the transfer is done — only to find out months later it never was.

RC Ready replaces guesswork with clarity.

## Why We Started With Maharashtra

The used-car market in India is booming, and Maharashtra is at the heart of it. Based on 2025 demand data, Maharashtra leads the country in second-hand car sales — with cities like Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, and Nashik driving record activity year after year.

### What's Driving the Growth

- **Tier-2 cities are leading the surge.** Over 62% of used-car demand now comes from cities outside the metros — places where reliable, plain-language guidance is hardest to find.
- **Metros stay strong.** Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore continue to post the highest absolute volumes.
- **Affordable models dominate.** Hatchbacks and sedans from Maruti Suzuki and Hyundai are the most-traded vehicles.

That combination — highest volume, biggest spread across cities, and a population most exposed to bad agents — is exactly why we built RC Ready for Maharashtra first. Get it right here, and it scales naturally to the rest of the country.

## What's Inside

### 1. The Transfer Wizard
A guided five-step journey from start to finish. Tell RC Ready who you are and what you're transferring, and it handles the sequence for you — no need to figure out the order yourself.

### 2. Readiness Audit
A smart checklist of more than 56 things you should verify before money changes hands. The list adapts to your situation:
- Buyer vs. seller
- Same-state vs. interstate transfer
- Loan vs. fully owned
- Same RTO vs. different RTO

You'll know — clearly — what's done, what's missing, and what's risky.

### 3. Document Scanner
Snap a photo of your RC, insurance, PUC certificate, or Aadhaar — RC Ready reads them and pulls out the details automatically. No retyping. No squinting at faded paper. It also flags expired insurance, mismatched chassis numbers, or missing endorsements.

### 4. Form Studio
Fills in the official RTO forms for you — Form 28, 29, 30, 32, 35, and 36 — using the details it has already gathered. Print-ready. No more downloading blank PDFs and writing the same information ten times.

### 5. Readiness Report
A final summary that scores how prepared you are, lists every red flag, and gives you a clean action plan for the RTO visit. Save it, share it with the other party, or hand it to your CA.

### 6. Deal History
Every transfer you start is saved on your device. Pause halfway, come back next week — it's all there.

## How To Use It

1. Open RC Ready in your browser.
2. Tell it whether you're buying or selling, and a few details about the car.
3. Upload photos of your documents (or type the details in manually if you prefer).
4. Walk through the checklist — RC Ready flags anything that looks off.
5. Print the filled forms it generates.
6. Take everything to the RTO with confidence.

That's it.

RC Ready is built carefully and reflects current rules to the best of our knowledge — but it is **not a substitute for legal advice**. Rules change. RTOs interpret things differently. When in doubt, confirm with your local RTO or a qualified professional. Use RC Ready as a guide, not a guarantee.

# Cars24

## Market Overview (2025 Data)
Maharashtra is the leading state for second-hand car sales in India, accounting for **over 20%** of total demand. This dominance is driven by high consumer demand and urban congestion in major hubs like Mumbai and Pune.

### Top Used Car Markets in India
| State | Demand Share |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Maharashtra** | ~20.7% |
| Karnataka | ~16.5% |
| Gujarat | ~13.5% |
| Uttar Pradesh | ~13.0% |
| Tamil Nadu | ~12.0% |

### Key Trends
*   **Tier-2 Cities**: Over **62%** of demand is now driven by Tier-2 cities.
*   **Metros**: Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore continue to maintain high sales volumes.
*   **Popular Models**: High demand for hatchbacks and sedans, particularly from **Maruti Suzuki** and **Hyundai**.
